FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How safe are your treatments for children and pets?
+
All our products are EPA-registered and carefully selected for minimal toxicity to mammals. We'll advise you on how long to stay out of treated areas (typically 1–2 hours). Our technicians are trained to apply treatments in a way that maximizes effectiveness while minimizing any risk to your family.
How long does a standard treatment take?
+
Most standard residential treatments take 45–90 minutes depending on property size and pest type. Larger properties or complex infestations (like termite treatments) may require 2–4 hours. We'll give you a time estimate before we begin.
What if pests come back after treatment?
+
We stand behind our work with a free revisit guarantee. If the same pest returns within the warranty period (30 days for single treatments, unlimited for quarterly plans), we'll return and re-treat at no charge. Your satisfaction is non-negotiable.
Do I need to leave my home during treatment?
+
For most treatments, we ask that occupants and pets vacate the treated areas for 1–2 hours to allow products to dry fully. For heat treatments or fumigation, longer absences may be needed. Your technician will provide clear instructions beforehand.
Do you offer commercial pest control services?
+
Absolutely. Our Sovereign plan is designed specifically for commercial clients including restaurants, warehouses, offices, and multi-unit buildings. We offer HACCP-compliant documentation, monthly scheduled visits, and 24/7 emergency response.
